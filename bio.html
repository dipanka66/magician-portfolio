Who is Dipanka Rajbongshi?

"I want to travel the world as much as I can in life and entertain people. Hope we leave everyone with a magical moment that they will never forget ..."

In Nalbari(Assam) born Dipanka Rajbongshi, who began his magic career at the age of 20 on receiving a deck of cards from his parents.


There was no looking back; Dipanka's talent was unmistakable. Entirely self- taught, he carried his cards with him everywhere, he learn basic magic from youtube and then he start to break down the senior magician's great magic.


As he continued practicing, his confidence and ability increased. He was praised by his family for his technique and hand craft at his home. He was able to create a curious situation among the students and friends of his institute.
